Health Pro Tips

Staying Healthy

  • Be physically active for at least 30 minutes most days of the week.
  • Eat a well-balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.

Writing Effective Queries

  • Be specific about your symptoms, including when they started and their severity.
  • Mention any relevant medical history or conditions you have.
  • List any medications or supplements you're currently taking.
  • Include lifestyle factors like diet, exercise habits, and stress levels.
  • Mention any treatments you've already tried and their effects.

When to Seek Immediate Help

  • Severe chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Sudden severe headache or confusion
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
  • Severe allergic reactions
  • For these symptoms, please call emergency services immediately rather than submitting a query.
